Coral Diseases We Detect

Our AI model can identify these coral conditions

🔴
Band Disease
High Severity

Characterized by a distinct band of tissue loss progressing across the colony.

⚪
Bleached Disease
Critical

Coral expels symbiotic algae due to stress, turning white.

💀
Dead Coral
Terminal

Complete loss of living tissue, only skeleton remains.

✅
Healthy Coral
Healthy

Vibrant coloration with active polyp extension and normal growth.

🟣
White Pox
High Severity

Irregular white patches caused by Serratia marcescens bacteria.

About

Why Coral Reef Monitoring Matters

Coral reefs are among the most biodiverse ecosystems on Earth, supporting over 25% of all marine species. However, they face unprecedented threats from climate change, pollution, and disease.

CoralGuard uses state-of-the-art YOLOv8 deep learning models to provide rapid, accurate detection of coral diseases and fish population counts, empowering researchers and conservationists with actionable data.
